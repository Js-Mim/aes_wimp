New Sonorities for Jazz Recordings: Separation and Mixing Using Deep Neural Networks.

This webpage provides sound examples related to the paper "New Sonorities for Jazz Recordings: Separation and Mixing Using Deep Neural Networks", S.I. Mimilakis, E. Cano, J. Abesser, G. Schuller, in Proc. of the 2nd AES Workshop on Intelligent Music Production, London, UK, 13 September 2016.
